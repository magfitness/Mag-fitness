The Coaching Experience

What coaching actually looks like.

A weekly call is only as valuable as what happens inside it. Here is what a 30-day coaching cycle delivers.

Week One

Foundation Call

A 30-minute video call to review your program, walk through technique on key lifts, set baseline measurements, and align on nutrition. You leave with a clear plan for the week ahead.

Week Two

Progress Call

Review your first week of training and nutrition data. Adjust load, volume, or meal structure based on real performance. Address any technique issues before they become habits.

Week Three

Mid-Cycle Call

Halfway through. We assess what is working, what is not, and recalibrate the back half of the program. This is where most clients break through their first plateau.

Week Four

Performance Call

Final review of the cycle. Re-test your benchmarks, measure progress against week one, and plan the next 30 days. Coaching clients typically continue into a second cycle.

Between Calls

Direct WhatsApp access for technique questions, nutrition adjustments, or anything that comes up. Responses within 24 hours, typically same-day.
